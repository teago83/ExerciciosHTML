Monster Hunter is a gaming franchise that started out in 2004 with a game called "Monster Hunter", released back then for the Playstation 2. Gathering many fans all over the world, the franchise got itself many releases, such as "Monster Hunter G", "Monster Hunter 2", "Monster Hunter Freedom Unite"... Going from the PS2, to the PSP, over to the 3DS and even to the Wii.
